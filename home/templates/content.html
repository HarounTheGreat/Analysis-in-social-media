Only here you can follow the opinions and comments of your followers on social networking sites moment by moment in an easy, effective and profitable way for time and money.

About US

Anbster is a website dedicated to analyzing comments and opinions about social media posts.
This site primarily targets businesses that rely on a social media marketing strategy, as it allows them to develop their products by identifying weaknesses their customers are complaining about and working to correct them.
method of work:
1- This site extracts the comments as they are and whatever their language.
2- Thanks to the artificial intelligence technology, it analyzes the comments and categorizes them into three groups: positive, negative and normal.
3- Finally, the result is presented to the user in a simple, clear and easy-to-read form.

How it works

Our services

Youtube

Our website can analyze comments about any video on YouTube and categorize them into three groups: neutral, positive and negative.
This is done by entering the full video link and choosingthe language and the number of comments.
Note:
Comments are automatically pulled according to the number of people who interact with them

Facebook

Our website can analyze comments about posts on the Facebook platform and categorize them into three groups: neutral, positive and negative.
This is done by entering your access token and the post_ID and choosing the language .
Note:
You can only analyze comments about your posts using your account on the pages that you are the administrator of.

Twitter

Our website can analyze Tweets about any account or any hashtag on Twitter and categorize them into three groups: neutral, positive and negative.
This is done by entering the account name,or the hashtag and choosing the language and the number of comments.
Note:
Tweets are automatically pulled according to when they were written.
